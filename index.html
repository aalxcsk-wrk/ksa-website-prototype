Pain Management

Lasting Relief. Without the Operating Table.

Chronic pain — whether from nerve conditions, spinal disorders or joint degeneration — affects millions of patients and represents one of the most complex challenges in modern medicine. KSA brings together a comprehensive range of minimally invasive pain management solutions, from precision radiofrequency systems to steerable epidural catheters, non-invasive neuromodulation and therapeutic phototherapy. All supported by our clinical specialists, in the room when it counts.

Key Advantages

Why Choose Minimally Invasive Pain Management?

Target the Source, Not the Symptom

Our solutions address pain at its origin — disrupting nerve signals, decompressing the spine or restoring tissue — rather than masking symptoms with long-term medication.

Minimally Invasive Across the Board

Every solution in this category is designed to minimise patient trauma — no open surgery, minimal anaesthesia, faster recovery and earlier return to daily life.

Repeatable When Clinically Needed

Unlike surgical intervention, these procedures can be repeated without cumulative risk — supporting long-term chronic pain management strategies for your patients.

Tumour Ablation

A Smarter Path to Minimally Invasive Treatment.

Thermal ablation uses precisely controlled radiofrequency or microwave energy to treat targeted tissue — without open surgery, without general anaesthesia, and with recovery times measured in hours, not weeks. KSA brings you a comprehensive range of RF and microwave ablation systems for tumour, thyroid and fibroid procedures, supported by our clinical specialists every step of the way.

Product availability is subject to local regulatory requirements. Contact us to confirm availability in your region.

Part of the Kromax Group
Our Partners
Key Advantages

Why Thermal Ablation?

Minimally Invasive

No open surgery, no general anaesthesia. Procedures performed through a thin needle under image guidance — reducing risk and patient discomfort significantly.

Organ-Sparing Precision

RF and microwave energy targets only the affected tissue — preserving healthy surrounding structures and eliminating the need for hormone replacement in most thyroid cases.

Faster Patient Recovery

Most patients return to normal activities within 24–48 hours. Hospitalisation time dramatically reduced compared to conventional surgery.

Repeatable When Needed

Thermal ablation can be performed again for additional or recurrent lesions — without the cumulative risks of multiple surgical procedures.
